# SummerTides Festival Database Presentation

## Slide 1: Project Overview
- SummerTides Festival hosts music, food, art, and sponsors across four main stages.
- Goal: design a relational database to manage performances, attendees, vendors, and sponsorships.

## Slide 2: Business Needs
- Track artist schedules and stage assignments.
- Manage sponsor contributions and stage sponsorship relationships.
- Record attendee ticket purchases and check-ins.
- Monitor vendor sales and revenue performance.

## Slide 3: Data Model
- Artists, Stages, Sponsors, Performances, Attendees, Vendors, VendorSales, StageSponsors.
- Primary keys and foreign keys enforce one-to-many and many-to-many relationships.
- Constraints ensure valid ticket types, performance days, and revenue values.

## Slide 4: Sample Queries
- Select artists and stage schedules.
- Filter VIP attendees and vendor categories.
- Aggregate ticket revenue and vendor sales.
- Join performances with artists and stages.
- Use views for VIP attendees, stage schedules, vendor revenue summaries, and sponsor stage assignments.

## Slide 5: Results and Delivery
- Completed relational schema with realistic sample data.
- Created reusable views for operational reporting.
- All scripts are organized by task and ready for execution.
- Next step: connect the database to a dashboard or event management app.
